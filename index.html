Arts

We offer all art subject courses in both O and A'level. These subjects include; Fine Art, History, Geography, Kiswahili, Agriculture, Computer, Literature in both English and Kiswahili, English, Christian Religious Education, Enterpreneurship and Economics

Sciences

We also offer various science sujects in both O and A'level. These subjects include; Biology, Physics, Mathematics and Chemistry.

DIT

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the school's all about?

The school is a private O and A'level mixed, day and boarding.

Where is Bright Academy located?

The school is located along Mbarara-Kasese road in Kamaiba ward in Central Division, Kasese Municipality.

What is the school's motto?

Treasure in Education.

What is the school's mission?

To create a model secondary school, A center of academic excellence.

What are the objectives of the school?

Through enforcing discipline among the students.
Provision of teaching and learning materials which aim at providing skills.
Recruitment of qualified staff.
To sensitize parents towards the increased enrolment of boys and girls.

What are the school's core values?

Discipline
Talent Oriented
Commitment
Godliness

What is the school's strategic focus?

Enhancing an institutional culture that embodies the school core values.
Ensuring provision of quality education.
Providing a service that would benefit all categories of people.
